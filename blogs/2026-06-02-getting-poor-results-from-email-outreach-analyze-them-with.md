This playbook demonstrates how to leverage AI tools like Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini to examine email campaign performance. The guide walks users through extracting Hunter Sequences data, organizing email content documentation, and running structured analyses to identify winning subject lines, effective recipient lists, and domain performance trends. The framework ultimately produces an actionable improvement plan grounded in industry benchmarks from Hunter's State of Email Outreach 2026 report.
